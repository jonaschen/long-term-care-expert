## Strategic Use of Grab Bars and Handrails

Installing grab bars and handrails provides the elder with essential points of contact for maintaining balance. You might consider placing these in high-risk areas such as the bathroom, along long hallways, and on both sides of any staircases. It is important that these bars are professionally installed and anchored securely to the wall to support the elder's weight. Unlike towel racks or door handles, dedicated grab bars are designed for a firm grip and heavy use. If you observe a behavioral pattern where the elder frequently touches the walls or leans on furniture while walking, it is a sign that installing a continuous handrail would be beneficial. Providing these steady points of support can greatly increase the elder's confidence and reduce the physical effort required to move safely through the home.
