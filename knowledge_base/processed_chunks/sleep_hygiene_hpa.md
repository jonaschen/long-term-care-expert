## Routine Habits
- **Consistency:** Maintain fixed wake-up and sleep times.
- **Delayed Bedtime:** Aim for 10-11 PM to avoid early morning waking.
- **Nap Limits:** Complete naps before 3 PM and keep them under 30 minutes.

## Environment Optimization
- **Sleep Only:** Use the bed only for sleep; avoid TV, phones, or eating in bed.
- **Comfort:** Keep the room cool, quiet, and dim. Use nightlights for toilet safety.
- **Anxiety Reduction:** Remove visible clocks to avoid "clock-watching" stress.

## Diet and Lifestyle
- **Fluid Control:** Reduce intake after dinner to minimize nocturia.
- **Stimulants:** Avoid caffeine, alcohol, and smoking near bedtime.
- **Sunlight:** Spend one hour outdoors in natural light to regulate the circadian rhythm.

## Psychological Strategies
- **Relaxation:** Establish a bedtime ritual (e.g., foot soak, breathing exercises).
- **Don't Force Sleep:** If awake after 20 minutes, leave the room for a relaxing activity until sleepy.
- **Worry Journal:** Write down concerns before bed to address them the next day.
