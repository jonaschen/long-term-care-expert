## Supporting Emotional Wellbeing Through Routine

A regular routine of movement is a powerful tool for maintaining a positive emotional state. Physical activity helps release natural tension and promotes a sense of calm. When an elder follows a consistent daily schedule—including times for light activity, rest, and social engagement—they often feel more secure and less overwhelmed by changes in their environment. Compared to the usual pattern of a sedentary day, a day with planned movement can significantly brighten the elder's outlook. Families can help by creating a supportive atmosphere where the elder feels encouraged to express how they feel. If the elder seems to be in low spirits, a gentle change of scenery, like a short walk in a park, can provide a helpful emotional lift.
