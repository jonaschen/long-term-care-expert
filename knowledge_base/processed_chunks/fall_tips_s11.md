## Maintaining Nighttime Safety

Nighttime movements present unique challenges due to low visibility and the potential for the elder to be less alert. You might consider placing a lamp or a light switch within easy reach of the bed so the elder never has to walk in the dark. Motion-sensor nightlights along the path to the bathroom are also very effective. Ensure that the floor is completely clear of obstacles like slippers or books before the elder goes to bed. If you observe that the elder seems more disoriented or unsteady during the night compared to their daytime pattern, it is even more important to have these environmental supports in place. Keeping a phone or an emergency call button by the bedside can provide extra peace of mind. Encouraging the elder to take their time and follow the slow-rising routine at night can further enhance their safety.
